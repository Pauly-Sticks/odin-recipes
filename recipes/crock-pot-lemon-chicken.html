Crock pot lemon chicken recipe

Description

My family keeps requesting this recipe over and over. It is a definite hit! A mix between a hearty potato stew and a lemony greek chicken make this a winner!

The measurements below are rough estimates. Add as much/little as you want and make it your own.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs chicken
  • 1 bag baby carrots (optional)
  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 1 box of chicken stock
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 can of artichoke hearts
  • Half a bottle of capers
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 container of feta cheese
  • 4 lemons
  • 1 container of greek yogurt
  • Chicken boullion to taste
  • black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Take chicken and marinate in the juice of two lemons and 1 cup of the greek yougurt for at least an hour.
  2. Chop the potatoes and onions into large bite sized pieces./li>
  3. Place the onions, potatoes, carrots (if you like carrots), chicken stock, garlic and the juice of two of the lemons into the crockpot and set on low.Do not add the chicken yet.
  4. Add the chicken boullion and pepper to taste.
    • You want it to be just under your liking salt wise.
    • The feta and capers will add more salty flavor later.
    • Getting the salt right at this point will keep the potatoes from being bland later.
    • The broth will not taste great. You are only checking for saltiness
    • Do not taste the broth if you already added the raw chicken.
  5. Now add the chicken and cover. Cook for 7 hours.
  6. Add the feta, capers and artichoke and cook for another half an hour.
  7. Add 1 cup of the greek yogurt and cook another 15 minuts.
  8. Shred the chicken, mix well and serve!
